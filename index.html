Privacy

Privacy policy

Data collection

Intake does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All capture history and settings stay on your Mac. API keys are stored in the macOS Keychain.

Update checks

Intake uses Sparkle to check for updates. This sends a request to GitHub to check for new versions. Sparkle may send your macOS version, app version, and hardware architecture as part of this request. No personally identifiable information is included.

iCloud sync

If enabled, Intake syncs settings across your Macs via iCloud. This data is governed by Apple's iCloud privacy policy. No capture content is synced â€” only configuration and destination settings.

Webhook destinations

If you configure a webhook destination (including Make.com, Zapier, or a custom URL), capture content is sent to that endpoint. Intake does not control what those services do with your data. No data is sent to external services unless you explicitly configure a webhook destination.

Analytics

Intake does not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry. No crash reports are sent automatically.
